Your notes

There are four main layers in box models. Content, padding, border and margins.

Content

This is where text and images reside. This is the most important part of the model.

Padding

This layer controls the distance from the box border. Use it to properly space content within.

Border

Surrounds the box. Divides the content from other contents.

Margin

This layer controls the distance of the model from other models. Use it to properly space models within the content.
